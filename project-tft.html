The Fair Tracker (BT3103)

The Problem

Shoppers often struggle to find the best deals for groceries across different supermarket chains. Price comparison is a tedious, manual process, making it difficult for consumers to consistently save money on their regular shopping lists.

The Solution

I contributed to the development of "The Fair Tracker", a web-based, centralized platform that provides transparent price comparisons across multiple supermarkets, aimed at streamlining the shopping experience for deal-seekers.

Platform Features

  • Real-time Price Comparison: Compare prices of grocery items across major supermarket chains instantly.
  • Grocery List Tracking: Users can create, save, and manage custom grocery lists to calculate total costs before visiting the store.
  • Favourites Function: Bookmark frequently bought items for quick price checking.
  • Store Locator: Integrated map feature to find the nearest supermarket branch carrying the required items at the best price.
